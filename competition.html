What is Business Science Arena?

Business Science Arena is a competition that takes place for students invited into our 180-Day Data Scientist Program (invitation only).

  • Each cohort progresses through a coaching system led by Matt Dancho and his team of coaches.
  • Students compete in order to advance their career.
  • The winner is revealed during program graduation.

To the victor goes the spoils...

The winner gets:

  • Immortality: Recognition by induction into our Business Science Arena Hall of Fame.
  • Special Certificate: The winner gains a special certificate offered only to the cohort winner. This can be added to your LinkedIn professional profile and portfolio website.
  • Video Testimonial: The winner gets a video testimonial from Matt Dancho attesting to their greatness.
  • A Special Prize: You'll need to join to learn about it.
